About Huang's Cafe — Hey there! I’m Helen. I made this webzine dedicated to me and also my parents. Since I grew up as a restaurant kid, I was always surrounded by my parents cooking and took it for granted. Now that I am in college, nothing can ever compare to their food. Realizing how important food means to me, I decided to make this webzine to honor the memories and also my parent’s hard work. I hope that I will come back to remember the recipes or just the fond memories related to the meal. I always had a hard time saying I love you to my parents so this is another way I wanted to show how grateful I am towards them. And here are some random pictures!
